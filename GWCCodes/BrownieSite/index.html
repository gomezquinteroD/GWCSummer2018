Brownie a la Mode

A simple step-by-step guide to making delicious brownies!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Mix wet ingredients.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Butter the pan.
  6. Pour the batter into the pan.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes until baked.
